# Architecture

## Site identity
Personal + professional hybrid with strong technical/project emphasis.

## Build philosophy
Build in layers:
1. structure
2. content
3. polish
4. animations
5. custom visuals

Avoid one-shot builds. Prefer small, modular commits.

## V1 pages
- Home
- About
- Projects
- Activities
- Contact

## Homepage goals
- strong personal first impression
- include photo
- highlight identity before work

## Design direction
- modern, clean, interactive
- not a generic AI portfolio
- reusable components
- scalable structure

## Constraints
- no heavy animation in V1
- no overdesign early
- prioritize maintainability
